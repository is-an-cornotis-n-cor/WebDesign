1958 - Tennis for two, the first video game ever released.

Tennis for two, a game made by William Higinbotham, was the first video game ever released. It was released in October 18, 1958. It is a game that simulated tennis, and similar to Pong, a game released in 1972. Tennis for two was first played on an oscilloscope screen (a circular computer screen) at the Visitors Day Instrumentation Division exhibit in 1958.

1962 - Spacewar!

Spacewar! was released in February 14, 1962. It was developed by Steve Russell. It was a very popular game and it was played on the PDP-1(Programmed Data Processor-1). Like the name, it was a space combat game that was similar to Space Invaders, a game released in 1978.

1967- The Brown Box

The Brown Box was the first video game console invented(first video game system that can be played on a television). It was licensed to Magnavox and released as the “Magnavox Odyssey” in 1972.

1972 - Pong

Pong was a game that was released in 1972. It was a phenomenal arcade game that was very popular. It was made by Allan Alcorn. In 1975 it was imported as a home game onto the Magnavox Odyssey.

1978 - Space Invaders

Space Invaders was a game released on Arcade systems and the NES, and it was the first fixed shooter. Space Invaders was made by Tomohiro Nishikado. Space Invader set the blueprint for the shooting genre.

1980 - Pac-Man

Pac-Man man was released on May 22, 1980, and it was released on Arcade systems.Pac-Man was made by Toru Iwatani.

1985 - NES

The NES (Nintendo Entertainment System) was a console that was released in North America in 1985. It was made by Masayuki Uemura, and it had improved color, sound, 8-bit graphics, and gameplay over all other consoles.

1989 - Gameboy

The gameboy was released by Nintendo in April 21, 1989. It was made by Gunpei Yokoi, and was one of the first handheld gaming devices. The gameboy was also bundled with the popular game Tetris, which made the gameboy even more popular.

1989 - Sega Genesis

The Sega Genesis was released in 1989 as one of the first 16-bit consoles. It was superior to the NES in many ways technologically, and with the popularity of the game Sonic the Hedgehog, It became a very popular console.

1992 - Mortal Kombat

Mortal Kombat was a game that was released in October 8, 1992. It was a game released in the arcade and had lots of blood and gore. This made Sega create the Videogame Rating Council in 1993 to rate video games. This rating system is still being used today. Mortal Kombat also had a movie released in 1993.
