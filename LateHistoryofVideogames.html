Video Game Systems

1996 - Nintendo 64

The Nintendo 64 was released in June 1996. Although It still used cartridges, it was the first ever 64 - bit console released.

2000 - Playstation 2

The Playstation 2 was one of the first 21st century consoles. It became the best-selling console of all time. It was also able to play games from the original Playstation.

2001 - Xbox

The Xbox was the first console released by Microsoft. It had lots of third party support, similar to Sony.

2005 - Xbox 360

The Xbox 360 was a step up from the Xbox. The console was released in November 22, 2005. It did very well, earning many Game Critic Awards.

2009 - Nintendo Wii

The Wii was released in November 19, 2009. It had motion-sensored remotes which helped it appeal to more people and help increase its popularity.

Present - Todays' consoles

Today we have consoles like the PS4 Pro the Xbox one X, and the Switch. People can even play games on their phones. Considering that video games were invented in 1954, it's almost impossible to tell the future of video games.
